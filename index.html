About NSSC

The NSSC (Nagpur Subsection Congress) is being hosted for the very first time by the newly established Nagpur Subsection.
The general notion of a Congress is to bring in potential speakers and listeners from all over the country to make the most out of an exhilarating 3 day event. From gaining an outlook on the latest technological advances to solving complex social issues and networking with the broadest array of enthusiastic young minds, we have everything you want. Come along my friend. Be a part of something phenomenally fantastic.
These action packed three day schedule will include some promising speakers delivering various diversified talks, interactive sessions, workshops and all about the glorious culture of Nagpur!
So take a note of the dates, people.It's going to be an unforgettable adventure.
Meet us at SB JAIN Institute of Technology, Management and Research , Nagpur from 10-12 March 2018 for an epic 3-day technological extravaganza that'll give you the best of takeaways, knowledge and experience. Let's take a step closer towards the future of technology!

IEEE


IEEE, being the world’s largest technical professional society, is continuously promoting the development and application of electrotechnology and allied sciences for the benefit of humanity and for the advancement of the profession. With the 4,30,000 members spanning across 165 countries of the globe, IEEE fosters the development of standards, that often became national and international standards and produces over 30% of the world’s literature in its related fields, publishing well over 100 peer - reviewed journals.

Nagpur Sub-Section


Coming into existance, The Nagpur Sub-Section was officially esatblished on 3rd September 2017. It consists of technical professionals as well as students from various institutions. The agenda behind the formation of this sub-section is to torch the paths of the engeering students and the professionals, to be ready enough for the competent world out there and to accept the globally posed challenges confidentally, via various events that we organize to update their knowledge and enhace their skill set.

S. B. Jain Institute of Technology, Management and Research

S. B. Jain Institute of Technology, Management & Research (SBJITMR), accredited by NAAC A grade, is the initiative of Shri Shantilalji Badjate, who devoted his lifetime to the field of education. His dream to build a world class educational infrastructure led to the beginning of Sir Shantilal Badjate Charitable Trust, Nagpur. SBJITMR is approved by AICTE (New Delhi) & DTE (Mumbai), is affiliated to Nagpur University (RTMNU) and is recognized by Government of Maharashtra. SBJITMR has carved a niche reputation for itself by imparting the quality education through highly qualified & experienced faculty members.
